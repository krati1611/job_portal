About the Position

India Post has invited applications for the Gramin Dak Sevak (GDS) recruitment for 2025. This is a golden opportunity for 10th pass candidates to secure a government job in the postal department. The role involves mail delivery, sale of postal products, and assisting in various postal operations in rural areas.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Age Limit: 18 to 40 years as on 01 January 2025 (Age relaxation applicable as per rules)
  • Educational Qualification: 10th Pass with Mathematics and English from a recognized board
  • Computer Knowledge: Basic computer training certificate of at least 60 days duration
  • Language Requirement: Must know the local language of the postal circle applied for
  • Nationality: Indian Citizen

Salary & Benefits

Branch Postmaster (BPM): Rs. 12,000 – 29,380

Assistant Branch Postmaster (ABPM) / Dak Sevak: Rs. 10,000 – 24,470

Additional Benefits: DA, stationary charges, travel allowance (where applicable)

Important Dates

  • Application Start Date: 25 July 2025
  • Last Date to Apply: 10 August 2025
  • Correction Window: 12 – 14 August 2025
  • Result Declaration: September 2025 (Tentative)

Selection Process

  1. Merit List: Based on marks obtained in 10th standard (no written exam)
  2. Document Verification: Verification of original documents and eligibility
  3. Final Selection: After successful verification and confirmation

Application Fee

  • Application Fee: Rs. 850
  • Payment Mode: Online through Credit Card / Debit Card / Net Banking or through any Head Post Office

How to Apply

  1. Visit the official website: indiapostgdsonline.gov.in
  2. Register using basic details (name, DOB, mobile number, etc.)
  3. Login and complete the application form
  4. Upload scanned documents (photo, signature, certificates)
  5. Pay the applicable fee (if any)
  6. Submit the form and take a printout for reference
