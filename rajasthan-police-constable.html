About the Position

The Rajasthan Police Department has announced recruitment for Constable positions across various districts of Rajasthan. This is a prestigious opportunity to serve in the state police force with attractive salary and benefits.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Age Limit: 18-23 years (Age relaxation as per government norms)
  • Educational Qualification: 10th/12th pass from recognized board
  • Physical Standards: Height - 168 cm (Male), 152 cm (Female)
  • Nationality: Indian Citizen
  • Language: Knowledge of Hindi and Rajasthani dialects

Salary & Benefits

Pay Scale: Level-3 (Rs. 21,700 - 69,100)

Grade Pay: Rs. 2,000

Additional Benefits: DA, HRA, Medical Allowance, Transport Allowance

Career Growth: Opportunities for promotion to higher ranks

Important Dates

  • Application Start Date: 15 July 2025
  • Last Date to Apply: 17 August 2025
  • Admit Card Release: September 2025
  • Written Exam Date: October 2025
  • Physical Test: November 2025

Selection Process

  1. Written Examination: Objective type questions on General Knowledge, Reasoning, etc.
  2. Physical Efficiency Test: Running, Long Jump, High Jump
  3. Medical Examination: Physical and medical standards verification
  4. Document Verification: Original documents verification

Application Fee

  • Application Fee: Rs. 750
  • Payment Mode: Online (Debit Card/Credit Card/Net Banking)

How to Apply

  1. Visit the official Rajasthan Police website
  2. Click on "Recruitment" section
  3. Find "Constable Bharti 2025" notification
  4. Click "Apply Online"
  5. Fill all required details carefully
  6. Upload scanned documents (Photo, Signature, etc.)
  7. Pay application fee
  8. Submit application and take printout
