About the Position

Chhattisgarh Professional Examination Board (CG Vyapam) has announced recruitment for Assistant Data Entry Operator (ADEO) positions. This is a technical position responsible for data entry and computer operations in various government departments.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Age Limit: 18-40 years (Age relaxation as per government norms)
  • Educational Qualification: 12th pass with Computer knowledge
  • Computer Skills: MS Office, Typing speed 30 WPM
  • Nationality: Indian Citizen
  • Residence: Must be resident of Chhattisgarh

Salary & Benefits

Pay Scale: Level-4 (Rs. 25,500 - 81,100)

Grade Pay: Rs. 2,400

Additional Benefits: DA, HRA, Medical Allowance

Career Growth: Opportunities for promotion to higher positions

Important Dates

  • Application Start Date: 25 August 2025
  • Last Date to Apply: 09 September 2025
  • Admit Card Release: October 2025
  • Written Exam Date: November 2025
  • Skill Test: December 2025

Selection Process

  1. Written Examination: General Knowledge, Computer Knowledge, Reasoning
  2. Typing Test: Computer typing test
  3. Document Verification: Original documents verification
  4. Medical Examination: Physical fitness check

Application Fee

  • Application Fee: Rs. 700
  • Payment Mode: Online (Debit Card/Credit Card/Net Banking)
