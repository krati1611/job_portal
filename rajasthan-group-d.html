About the Position

Rajasthan Government has announced recruitment for Group D positions across various departments. These are entry-level positions in government offices, schools, hospitals, and other government institutions.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Age Limit: 18-40 years (Age relaxation as per government norms)
  • Educational Qualification: 8th pass minimum
  • Physical Standards: Good physical fitness
  • Nationality: Indian Citizen
  • Residence: Must be resident of Rajasthan

Salary & Benefits

Pay Scale: Level-1 (Rs. 18,000 - 56,900)

Grade Pay: Rs. 1,800

Additional Benefits: DA, HRA, Medical Allowance

Job Security: Permanent government job

Important Dates

  • Application Start Date: 05 October 2025

  • Admit Card Release: November 2025
  • Written Exam Date: December 2025
  • Document Verification: January 2026

Selection Process

  1. Written Examination: General Knowledge, Reasoning, Basic Mathematics
  2. Physical Test: Basic physical fitness test
  3. Document Verification: Original documents verification
  4. Medical Examination: Physical fitness check

Application Fee

  • Application Fee: Rs. 900
  • Payment Mode: Online (Debit Card/Credit Card/Net Banking)
