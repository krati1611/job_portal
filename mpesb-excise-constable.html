About the Position

Madhya Pradesh Employee Selection Board (MPESB) has announced recruitment for Excise Constable positions in the Excise Department. These positions are responsible for enforcing excise laws and regulations in the state.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Age Limit: 18-25 years (Age relaxation as per government norms)
  • Educational Qualification: 12th pass from recognized board
  • Physical Standards: Height - 168 cm (Male), 155 cm (Female)
  • Nationality: Indian Citizen
  • Residence: Must be resident of Madhya Pradesh

Salary & Benefits

Pay Scale: Level-3 (Rs. 21,700 - 69,100)

Grade Pay: Rs. 2,000

Additional Benefits: DA, HRA, Medical Allowance, Transport Allowance

Career Growth: Opportunities for promotion to higher ranks

Important Dates

  • Application Start Date: 08 August 2025
  • Last Date to Apply: 22 August 2025
  • Admit Card Release: September 2025
  • Written Exam Date: October 2025
  • Physical Test: November 2025

Selection Process

  1. Written Examination: General Knowledge, Reasoning, Mathematics
  2. Physical Efficiency Test: Running, Long Jump, High Jump
  3. Medical Examination: Physical and medical standards verification
  4. Document Verification: Original documents verification

Application Fee

  • Application Fee: Rs. 800
  • Payment Mode: Online (Debit Card/Credit Card/Net Banking)

How to Apply

  1. Visit the official MPESB website
  2. Click on "Recruitment" section
  3. Find "Excise Constable Bharti 2025" notification
  4. Click "Apply Online"
  5. Register with basic details
  6. Fill application form with all required information
  7. Upload scanned documents (Photo, Signature, etc.)
  8. Pay application fee
  9. Submit application and take printout
